  • EMEGUA,ZIGORA & MERBEBT

    Emegua, Zigora and Merbebt are books written by Ethiopian author Alemayehu Wasie. These books revolve around the hidden spiritual secrets and Ehhiopian long story to the ancient religious places and monsteries in the country. It has three sequels. In this sophisticated age of space exploration, Alemayehu Wasie tried to express his own look and experience towards the religious secrets vividly. These books adequately capable of deserving the eyes of all readers. Written with a flawless literature coupled with a magnanimous storytelling, it has a particular style that is unique only to the author. Chapter after chapter Emegua takes you on a voyage overflowing with suspense. Its breathtaking story follows along, as we turn each page. It manifests a narrative scene.
